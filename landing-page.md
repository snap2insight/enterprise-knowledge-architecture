# Enterprise Knowledge Architecture

A way to set the highest bar for how a team captures, reviews, and shares
its knowledge — built for AI agents and human contributors working side
by side.

## Why it matters

Every organization carries more knowledge than any one person can hold.
Increasingly, that knowledge flows through AI assistants as much as
through people. Most teams haven't yet asked: *who can see what, on
what basis, and for how long?*

This is a small, opinionated way to answer those questions — without
slowing anyone down.

## What you'll find

A guided tour you can read in about ninety minutes; a worked example
you can copy; and a checklist your auditors will recognize.

## Who it's for

Engineering and security leaders who care about doing this well — and
would rather not choose between *moving fast* and *being audit-ready*.
